Welcome to Rajarshi Shahu Mahavidyalaya, Latur

Affiliated with SRTMU (Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University)

Rajarshi Shahu Mahavidyalaya, Latur (Autonomous) is premier institution committed to academic excellence, innovation,and the holistic development of students. With a strong foundation in quality education, modern infrastructure, and a student-focused learning environment, the college provides opportunities that help learners grow intellectually, professionally, and socially.The instution believes in shaping not just successful careers, but responsible and confident individuals. Through innovative teaching methods, research-oriented learning, and a culture of ethical values, students are encouraged to think critically, explore creativity, and contribute meaningfully to society.At Rajarshi Shahu Mahavidyalaya, education goes beyond textbooks, We strive to nurture leadership, lifelong learning, and social responsibility, empowering students to face global challenges and become the leaders of tomorrow.
